ສະບາຍດີ, this app is meant for students and teachers of the medical English program at the University of Health Sciences in Vientiane, Laos. There are two primary views, one for students and one for teachers.

Why is it important? Being able to carry a casual conversation in English isn’t enough for a Lao medical professional. They will be coming across terms like “electroencephalogram”. We might not teach them that word specifically, but we teach them the meaning of electro-(prefix), encephalo(root), and -gram(suffix). Knowing the many “parts” of medical terminology maximizes their ability to dissect complex words, and accurately understand their meaning.

Students can search for vocabulary by the class(year 1, 2, or 3) and part(prefix, root, suffix) filters.

Teachers can add words to the database. There are very few teachers that will be adding words, they will be given the “secret” word to complete the submission.

This app is a resource meant to compliment a new curriculum that is in development. The database will be updated as the curriculum is updated, and any placeholder text will be replaced. The app will eventually be primarily Lao in language. ໂຊກດີ!
